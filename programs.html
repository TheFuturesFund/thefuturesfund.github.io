OUR PROGRAMS

We teach youth a combination of the technical and soft skills necessary to join the workforce and contribute to the local economy.

DIGITAL ARTS

We teach introductory computer coding languages, including HTML, JavaScript, and Ruby. This program leads to paid internships with local web development companies.

PERFORMING ARTS

We teach basic playing techniques for stringed instruments. This program leads to create of music ensembles that students are paid to play publicly.

LITERARY ARTS

We teach youth to go into their neighborhoods to pull out stories of personal and cultural significance. The program leads to the creation of books published by youth.

VISUAL ARTS

We teach kids to capture and share stories through powerful imagery. This program leads to the creation of prints featured in local galleries.
