• DIGITAL

    We teach basic computer programming skills to youth.
    This program leads to paid internships with local web development companies.

  • VISUAL

    We teach documentary photography skills to youth.
    This program leads to the creation of prints featured in local galleries & sold publicly.

  • PERFORMING

    We teach classical musicianship skills to youth.
    This program leads to the creation of music ensembles that are paid to perform publicly.

  • LITERARY

    We teach ethnographic storytelling skills to youth.
    This program leads to creation of books featured in local bookstores & sold publicly.
